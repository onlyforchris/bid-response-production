# Format Baseline

Use this only when the tender file or customer template does not specify formatting.

## Page

- Paper: A4 portrait
- Margins: top 2.5 cm, bottom 2.5 cm, left 2.8 cm, right 2.6 cm
- Gutter: 0
- Header: 1.5 cm
- Footer: 1.75 cm
- Page number: centered footer, preferably `第 X 页 / 共 Y 页`

## Text

- Body: 宋体小四, black
- First-line indent: 21 pt
- Paragraph spacing: before 0 pt, after 5 pt
- Line spacing: 1.5

## Headings

- Level 1: 宋体三号, bold, black; centered or aligned to the customer template; before 12 pt, after 6 pt; keep with next
- Level 2: 宋体四号, bold, black; left aligned; before 8 pt, after 4 pt; keep with next
- Level 3: 宋体小四, bold, black; left aligned; before 6 pt, after 3 pt
- Numbering: `1`, `1.1`, `1.1.1`
- TOC: generated from heading styles, levels 1-3, right-aligned page numbers with dot leaders

## Figures

- Images: centered, preserve aspect ratio
- Figure captions: below image, centered, 宋体五号, format `图 X-X 说明文字`
- Keep image and caption on the same page when possible

## Tables

- Table text: 宋体小四, black
- Line spacing: 1.5
- Cell vertical alignment: middle
- Header: bold, light gray 15% shading
- Borders: outer 1 pt, inner 0.5 pt, black or automatic
- Long tables: repeat header row across pages
- Do not inherit historical 1.0 table line spacing

## Normalization Rules

- Do not keep 仿宋 body text unless the tender/customer template explicitly requires it.
- Do not keep 12 pt body text as a default.
- Do not mix Chinese numbering and Arabic numbering unless the target template already requires it.
